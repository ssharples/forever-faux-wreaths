## Content Guidelines

### Blog Post Structure
```
1. SEO Title (60 chars max, include primary keyword)
2. Meta Description (155 chars, include keyword + CTA)
3. H1 Heading (matches title)
4. Introduction (100-150 words, hook + keyword)
5. Table of Contents (for posts 1000+ words)
6. Main Content (H2/H3 sections, 800-1500 words)
7. FAQ Section (3-5 questions with schema)
8. CTA Section (link to shop/bespoke)
9. Related Posts (internal links)
```

### Image Requirements
- Featured image: 1200x630px (social sharing)
- In-content images: Alt text with keywords
- Compress all images (WebP format preferred)

### Internal Linking Rules
- Every post links to at least 2 product pages
- Every post links to 1-2 other blog posts
- Every seasonal post links to relevant collection page
- Every funeral/memorial post links to bespoke service
